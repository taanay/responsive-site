Estadio Santiago Bernabéu

The Home Ground

The Estadio Santiago Bernabéu is a world-renowned soccer stadium located in Madrid, Spain. It is the home stadium of the legendary soccer club, Real Madrid. The stadium has a seating capacity of 81,044 and is known for its impressive architecture and design. In recent years, there have been plans to modernize and expand the stadium further, with the aim of improving the fan experience and generating more revenue. know more, about the club.

14 Times European Champions

There has only been one team in the history of the UEFA Champions League to win the competition three times in a row. That team is Real Madrid. They achieved this feat by winning the tournament in the 2015-16, 2016-17, and 2017-18 seasons.

The manager who led them to these victories was Zinedine Zidane. Zidane is widely regarded as one of the greatest footballers of all time, and he has also become a successful manager, especially during his time at Real Madrid.
